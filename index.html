From the Superintendent

In partnership with parents, students, and the community, Hamilton District 328 intends to provide the best education and extra-curricular activities in the tri-state area with special emphasis on current educational practices and the application of technology.

Dear Parents, Students, and Other Stakeholders in the Hamilton Community,

Thank you for visiting our web page. Please make an effort to visit our website periodically because we will be continually updating information. You will also find board minutes, agendas, budgets, and a variety of listings of activities. The mission statement above is what guides the decisions we make with our students. We invite you to join us on this journey. Your feedback is important to us. Please feel free to contact me at (866) 332-3880. I look forward to hearing from you.

Mr. Joe Yurko

Superintendent, Hamilton District 328
